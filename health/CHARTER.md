# health Charter

## 1. Mission Statement

**health** is a comprehensive health monitoring and wellness platform designed to track, analyze, and improve personal and organizational health metrics. The mission is to provide privacy-first health tracking that empowers users with insights into their physical and mental well-being—enabling data-driven health decisions while maintaining strict privacy controls and data ownership.

The project exists to democratize health data analysis—making sophisticated health tracking accessible to individuals while ensuring their most sensitive data remains private and secure.

---

## 2. Tenets (Unless You Know Better Ones)

### Tenet 1: Privacy Above All

Health data most sensitive. End-to-end encryption. User owns data. No data selling. No unauthorized access. Privacy non-negotiable.

### Tenet 2. User Control

User controls their data. Export anytime. Delete anytime. Granular sharing. Opt-in everything. No surprises.

### Tenet 3. Evidence-Based Insights

Recommendations backed by research. No pseudoscience. Clear uncertainty. Credible sources. Medical disclaimer. Science first.

### Tenet 4. Integration Without Lock-In

Integrate with wearables, apps, services. Apple Health. Google Fit. Garmin. Whoop. But data always exportable. No ecosystem trap.

### Tenet 5. Holistic View

Physical health. Mental health. Sleep. Nutrition. Activity. Stress. Connected picture. Not isolated metrics.

### Tenet 6. Actionable, Not Overwhelming

Insights lead to action. Not data overload. Prioritized recommendations. Clear next steps. Sustainable habits.

### Tenet 7. Inclusive Design

All abilities. All ages. All health levels. Accessible UI. Inclusive language. No judgment. Everyone welcome.

## 3. Scope & Boundaries

### In Scope

**Health Tracking:**
- Activity tracking
- Sleep monitoring
- Nutrition logging
- Heart rate variability
- Stress indicators
- Mood tracking

**Data Integration:**
- Wearable device sync
- Health app integration
- Medical device integration (with safeguards)
- Manual entry support

**Analysis & Insights:**
- Trend analysis
- Correlation discovery
- Anomaly detection
- Personalized insights
- Health scoring

**Privacy & Security:**
- End-to-end encryption
- Local-first architecture
- Granular permissions
- Audit logging
- Compliance (HIPAA, GDPR)

**Reporting:**
- Personal dashboards
- Health reports
- Export capabilities
- Sharing (opt-in)

### Out of Scope

- Medical diagnosis (use healthcare providers)
- Prescription management (use medical systems)
- Emergency services (call 911)
- Insurance integration (use appropriate channels)
- Social features (focus on individual health)

### Boundaries

- Health companion, not medical device
- Insights, not diagnosis
- Tracking, not treatment
- User-controlled, not platform-controlled
