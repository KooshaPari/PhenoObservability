# Support

## Getting help

- **Bug reports & feature requests:** [Open an issue](https://github.com/KooshaPari/PhenoObservability/issues/new/choose).
- **Security vulnerabilities:** See [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md) for private reporting.
- **Questions & discussion:** Open a GitHub issue with the `question` label.

## Scope

`PhenoObservability` is maintained on a best-effort basis by the
[Phenotype](https://github.com/KooshaPari) org. Response times are not
guaranteed. For commercial support, contact the maintainers directly.
